Corn Pudding

The epitome of Thanksgiving comfort food!

I find that my Thanksgiving celebrations are never complete without corn pudding; it's a must-have among my friends and family.

Allrecipes said it best: "This creamy corn pudding recipe is a little sweet, a little savory, and totally delicious." Give this recipe a try; you won't be disappointed!

Ingredients

  • 5 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted and slightly cooled
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 2 (14.75 ounce) cans cream-style corn

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 2-quart casserole dish.
  2. Whisk eggs lightly in a large bowl. Add milk, melted butter, sugar, and cornstarch; whisk until well combined. Stir in drained corn and cream-style corn until fully blended. Pour mixture into the prepared casserole dish.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 1 hour.
